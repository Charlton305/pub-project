About us

The White Horse is a local village pub and restaurant located in Maidstone, Kent. We are dedicated to our work and strive to provide a warm and welcoming environment. Our aim is to ensure all our customers leave with a smile on their face and want to come back again and again. Our staff, who are highly trained and motivated, along with Landlords Paul and Alison Hood who have over 35 years of experience in the hospitality industry, will make sure your visit is one to remember.
